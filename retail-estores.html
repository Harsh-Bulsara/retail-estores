Retail E-Stores

The retail sector refers to the part of the economy that involves the sale of goods or services directly to consumers for their personal use. It encompasses a wide range of businesses and industries, from brick-and-mortar stores to online retailers, supermarkets, department stores, specialty stores, and more. Nowasys offers a wide range of services to the retail sector. The company's solutions are designed to help retailers improve their operations, increase sales, and provide a better customer experience. Nowasys' services include

Retail services

Business intelligence and analytics

Business Intelligence (BI) and Analytics transform data into insights, improving decision-making, predicting trends, and enhancing business growth efficiently

Point of Sale (POS) Systems

Point of Sale (POS) systems streamline transactions by managing sales, inventory, and payments, enhancing business efficiency and customer experience.

Retail & eCommerce

Hyper-personalized recommendation engines, dynamic pricing optimization, and customer retention models enhance user experience, maximize revenue, and improve customer loyalty.

Customer Relationship Management (CRM)

Customer Relationship Management (CRM) systems help businesses manage interactions, improve customer engagement, and boost sales through data-driven insights.

Online Presence

Online presence strengthens brand visibility, attracts customers, and builds credibility through websites, social media, and digital marketing.

Supply chain management

Supply chain management optimizes logistics, reduces costs, and ensures efficient product delivery through streamlined processes and real-time tracking.

AI-based Retail Apps

Nowasys offers a variety of AI-based retail apps that can help retailers improve their operations, increase sales, and provide a better customer experience. These apps use artificial intelligence to provide retailers with insights into customer behavior, personalize recommendations, and automate tasks.

IoT based Retail Software

Nowasys also offers a variety of IoT based retail software that can help retailers improve their operations, increase sales, and provide a better customer experience. These apps use Internet of Things (IoT) devices to collect data about customer behavior in stores. This data can be used to improve the customer experience, such as by providing personalized recommendations or by sending alerts when a customer is near a product they have previously expressed interest in.

Machine Learning Chatbots

Nowasys also offers a variety of machine learning chatbots that can help retailers improve their customer service. These chatbots use artificial intelligence to simulate human conversation. Chatbots can be used to provide customer service, answer questions, and make recommendations. This can help retailers to improve customer service and reduce costs

Big Data Retail Analytics Apps

Nowasys also offers a variety of big data retail analytics apps that can help retailers improve their operations, increase sales, and provide a better customer experience. These apps use large sets of data to gain insights into customer behavior, sales trends, and inventory levels. This data can be used to improve decision-making, such as by identifying products that are likely to be popular or by identifying stores that are underperforming.
